Refund and cancellation policy

Pip is billed monthly through Stripe at $100 per month unless a valid discount is applied at checkout.

Cancellations

Customers may request cancellation by contacting CharlieSystems support. Cancellation stops future renewal charges. Access may remain active through the end of the current paid billing period unless otherwise stated in writing.

Refunds

Refund requests are reviewed case by case. If a customer cannot access the software because of a verified licensing or installation issue that CharlieSystems cannot reasonably resolve, CharlieSystems may issue a refund for the affected billing period.

Non-refundable items

Completed monthly subscription periods, custom onboarding work, and usage after successful license activation are generally not refundable except where required by law or agreed by CharlieSystems.

Billing support

For billing, cancellation, or refund questions, contact support@charliesystems.net.
