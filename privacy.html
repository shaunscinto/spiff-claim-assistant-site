Policy

Privacy policy

CharlieSystems operates Spiff Claim Assistant, a Windows desktop software service for reviewing spiff claim opportunities from paid invoice records.

Information collected

CharlieSystems may collect information needed to provide the service, including customer name, billing email, license key, license status, subscription status, support messages, and basic device activation records.

Payment information

Payments are processed by Stripe. CharlieSystems does not store full card numbers or card security codes. Stripe may provide billing status, customer identifiers, subscription identifiers, and receipt information needed to manage access.

Desktop app data

Portal credentials and invoice processing records are intended to remain on the customer’s local machine unless the customer sends files or support details to CharlieSystems for troubleshooting.

How information is used

Information is used to provide software access, manage licenses, process billing, respond to support requests, investigate technical problems, and maintain service security.

Sharing

CharlieSystems does not sell customer personal information. Information may be shared with service providers such as Stripe where needed for payment processing, licensing, hosting, security, or support.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@charliesystems.net.
